Favorite Sport: Soccer

Soccer (football, if you're anywhere outside the US) is my favorite sport to watch and play. I love the pace of the game, the strategy behind build-up play, and how a single moment can completely change a match.

Whether it's a premier league game or a Champions League final, the atmosphere and unpredictability keep me hooked.

What I love most

  1. The ninety minutes of nonstop momentum shifts
  2. Watching a well-drilled team build an attack from the back
  3. The unpredictability — any team can have their day
