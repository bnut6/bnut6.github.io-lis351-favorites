Favorite Movie: The Dark Knight

The Dark Knight is my favorite movie, no contest. Christopher Nolan's 2008 Batman film plays more like a crime thriller than a typical superhero movie, and Heath Ledger's performance as the Joker is one of the best in film history.

What makes it stand out is how it treats Batman's choices as genuinely difficult. The practical stunts and the way Chicago stands in for Gotham give it a grounded, realistic feel most superhero movies don't have.

Top 3 reasons it's my favorite

  1. Heath Ledger's Joker — one of the best performaces in cinema
  2. Practical stunts and real locations instead of heavy CGI
  3. A script that treats its villain as a genuine philosophical threat

Film details

Released in 2008, directed by Christopher Nolan, and part of The Dark Knight Trilogy. You can read more on the IMDb page for The Dark Knight.
