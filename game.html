Favorite Game: Elden Ring

Elden Ring is my favorite video game of all time. Developed by FromSoftware, it drops you into the Lands Between, a massive open world packed with secrets, brutal boss fights, and real freedom in how you build your character.

What sets it apart from other open-world games is that it doesn't hold your hand. You can wander almost anywhere from the start and figure things out yourself. Also a quick fun fact, I have over 650 hours on the game.

Why I love it

  • Total freedom to explore in any direction from the very beginning
  • Boss fights that are hard but always feel fair once you learn the pattern
  • A huge variety of weapons and builds — no two playthroughs feel the same

Quick specs

Developer
FromSoftware
Genre
Action role-playing game
Platforms
PC, PlayStation, Xbox
